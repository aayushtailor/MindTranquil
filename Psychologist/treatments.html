Common Conditions We Treat

We use assessment-driven insights to tailor the right approach for you.

Anxiety & Stress

Panic, worry, sleep issues, physical tension, avoidance.

Depression

Low mood, loss of interest, fatigue, hopelessness, appetite change.

OCD & Phobias

Obsessions/compulsions, fears (heights, social, health, etc.).

Child Behavior

Tantrums, attention issues, developmental concerns.

Learning & Academic

Reading/writing/math gaps, exam anxiety, study planning.

Neuropsych Needs

Attention, memory, executive functions, return-to-work/school.

Approach

Evidence-Based Methods

Interventions selected from your test profile—no one-size-fits-all.

CBT (Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy)

Restructure unhelpful thoughts and build healthy behavior patterns with practical tools.

ACT & Mindfulness

Acceptance-based skills to reduce struggle and increase values-based actions.

Behavioral & Parent Training

For child behavior—simple routines, reinforcement, and calm strategies at home and school.

Study & Skills Coaching

Executive-function tools: planning, time-blocking, recall strategies, test-taking.

Relapse Prevention

Booster sessions and check-ins to maintain gains and confidence.

Multidisciplinary Handover

Coordination with psychiatry, speech/OT, or school counselors when needed.

Simple Daily Practices

  • 2-minute breathing reset every 3 hours
  • Sleep routine: fixed wake time + low-light 1 hr before bed
  • Movement snack: 10 min walk/stretch twice daily
  • Thought log: capture + reframe one worry per day
